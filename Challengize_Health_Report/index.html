Challenges That Work

How the exclusive Challengize Health Points can generate insights

Our Challenges are not just super fun and engaging. They are also an incredibly helpful tool for HR and managers to understand how motivated their teams are. We do it by using our Health Points, based on a Wellness Status Survey. We ask our participants how they feel regarding physical health, physical activity and general wellness. The survey is applied several times during a Challenge, so we can track the progression.

Let’s take a look at some results from two current clients. PRVård is a homecare company and Norrmejerier a leading dairy producer in the north of Sweden.

The Challenges

Norrmejerier ran their first ever Challenge for a 9 week period with 197 employees divided into 25 teams across the entire company. PR Vård engaged all their employees with 7 teams for a short 4 week Challenge.

How did they do?


The chart on the right shows the point increase when aggregating all the survey dimensions after their Challenge. The higher the number, the greater the increase in the team’s overall well-being.

As you can see a majority of the teams had a considerable increase in their well-being, with the top team increasing almost 60%!

Also, for the HR manager it is clear where more attention is needed: on the few teams that had a decrease on well-being. So if given only one hour to spend on proactive health, you know exactly where it will have the greatest impact.

What About Individual Dimensions?

Another benefit of our Health Points report is that they are broad. So you can identify exactly which dimension you need to pay more attention to. Is it general wellbeing? Is it health? Is it physical activities? And finding this is a helpful way to make your employees happier and more engaged. The next graph shows how the 3 dimensions progressed during the Challenge.
