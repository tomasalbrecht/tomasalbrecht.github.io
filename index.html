About Me.

Hi! My name is Tomás. I'm born and raised in Brazil, but currently living in Stockholm, where I am studying at KTH's Interacive Media Technology Masters Program. Back in Brazil, I was working as a strategic copywriter, with projects for many different brands. I also like research to reasearch in topics like social media, digital humanities, and the intersection between society and technology :)

Research

I was born curious. Therefore, it is natural for me to like academic research. This are a couple of papers I have written.

CHILDHOOD 2.0

In my Bachelor Thesis, I explored how the use of Facebook is affecting how kids socialize and make friendships. I used an intersection between communication theory, sociology, psychology and social media. It was selected and presented in 2015's Comunicon, the International Congress in Communication and Consume, in São Paulo.

VIGILANTE

After my Bachelor, I did a 2 year specialization on Digital Culture and Social Media, in the Vale dos Sinos University, Brazil. My final project is an article on how the Internet, once envisioned as a democratizing tool is turning into a privacy threatning system.

Work

My Bachelor is in Marketing and Communications, and I have always been interested in strategic creativity. I have worked for some big brands in Brazil, such as the beer brands from Ambev (Brahma, Skol, Polar), fashion retailers (Renner) and Broadcasters. My role was always between the creation and the planning departments.


Currently I work as the Digital Manager for Challengize, a Swedish Start Up that develop health challenges for business. We have worked with amazing clients such as Stadium, SJ (Swedish Train System), Stora Enso and others. My role is to take care of all the digital marketing, which include Social Media strategy and Google AdWords management.


I also volunteer in a Engineers Without Borders project, on which we develop and build wind turbines in Tanzania, and right now I work as a official student blogger for KTH.
